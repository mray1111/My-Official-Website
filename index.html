We organize a Mega Plantation drive to make the campus more beautiful than before. As we know, IIT Guwahati is one of the greenest and most beautiful campuses in India. We take care of it and ensure that its greenery never fades away.

This project delves into the assessment of the carbon footprint of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati campus, examining its environmental impact based on greenhouse gas emissions (CO2). The study aligns with India's commitment to climate action unveiled during the COP26 summit, emphasizing the crucial role of technical education institutions in mitigating carbon emissions. The primary objective is to classify the campus as Carbon Positive, Carbon Negative, or Carbon-Neutral.
